Why Organic Food

For food to be categorised as organic food, it needs to be produced without the use of synthetic methods such as chemical fertilizers or synthetic pesticides.

Avoiding these synthetic methods in organic produce enables organic farmers to yield only natural and gm-free fruits and vegetables as well as best quality meat. Furthermore, organic food is environment-friendly as it reduces pollution and greenhouse gas emission during food production. Local wildlife is not affected by organic farms as opposite to those that use artificial fertilizers and organically farmed animals are given enough space to naturally thrive and grow.

We choose only organic food because our customers and their welfare are important to us. That is why strive to deliver only natural, environmentally friendly and best quality food.
